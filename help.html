Welcome to the CMU Shuttle and Escort App!

Directions:
1. Enter your current location or desired start location in the "Start Location" text box. Click "set" to set this as the start location.
2. Next, enter your destination in the "Enter Destination" text box.
3. Now hit "Get Directions". An alerty text box will pop up, tell you how to use the shuttle to get to your desired destination.
